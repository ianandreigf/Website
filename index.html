About Me

I'm an assistant professor at the Department of mathematics of the National University of Singapore.

I did my Ph.D. in mathematics at UC Berkeley. My advisor was Sug Woo Shin.

I was also a postdoctoral researcher working in the arithmetic geometry group Arithmetische Algebraische Geometriehere. of Universität Bonn.

You can find a copy of my CV here, and most likely here.

Research key words:

Perfectoid spaces, Diamonds, v-sheaves, Stacks of local Shtukas, Shimura varieties, Categorical local Langlands.
