Ian Gleason (Universität Bonn)

I'm a postdoctoral researcher at Universität Bonn, working in the arithmetic geometry group (Arithmetische Algebraische Geometriehere.). I studied my Ph.D. in mathematics at UC Berkeley. My advisor was Sug Woo Shin. My research interests are in p-adic geometry and number theory. More precisely the theory of diamonds and v-sheaves, local Shimura varieties and moduli spaces of p-adic shtukas.

You can find a copy of my CV here, and most likely here.
